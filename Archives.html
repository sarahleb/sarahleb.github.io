Archives

The #Places that Matter

No repository is perfect. However, both of these have come pretty close for me. They are, by and large, the archives that have influenced me the most in my career so far. Looking forward to adding more!

The Bentley Historical Library

This is where I currently work. The Bentley Historical Library holds collections from both the state of Michigan as well as the University of Michigan. I've been fortunate enough to work with a few collections here, including the Dudley Randall papers, the Lewis Family papers, and the Center for the Education of Women records.

The UWC Robben Island Mayibuye Archives

This is a fantastic archive located in Cape Town, South Africa. The material focuses on the liberation struggle during the apartheid era, particularly the papers of individuals who were imprisoned for political reasons at the Robben Island prison (now Museum). Aliza Elkin, Ève Bourbeau-Allard, and I worked with staff there to standardize processing guidelines.
